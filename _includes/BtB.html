Bike the Bullet

"Bike The Bullet" is a high-intensity bullet hell game inspired by streamer CDawgVA and his cyclathon cast, created for Monkey Jam 2024 under a harrowing 3-day time constraint. Leading a team of 4, I established a task list and timeline to manage the project efficiently. The development required writing systems breakdowns, designing game balance and gameplay loops, and making art and sound design decisions, alongside a ton of programming, done mostly by me.
